Michael Joseph Jackson

The greatest pop-star in history.

Below is a time line of Michael Jackson' life:

  • 1958 - Early life in Gary, Indiana
  • 1964 - Professional debut with his elder brothers (The Jackson 5)
  • 1971 - Solo career with Motown Records
  • 1979 - First album (Off the Wall)
  • 1986 - Changing appearance, tabloids and films
  • 1993 - First child sexual abuse accusations and first marriage
  • 1995 - Second marriage and fatherhood
  • 1997 - Label dispute
  • 2002 - Second child abuse allegations, trial and acquittal
  • 2009 - Death
